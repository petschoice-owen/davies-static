134 Years and Still Going Strong!

Established in 1881, the Original Davies range of products has been developed with over 100 years' experience. By listening to their customers' needs and using this experience in conjunction with new manufacturing techniques, Davies have been able to deliver wholesome, nutritious foods that also move with the times. From Chub Rolls to Chomping Chews, the Davies range has evolved over the years and they are pleased to announce the launch of new Davies Grain Free Complete Dog Food.

The Davies range of complete pet food is manufactured in Lancashire. The company was started by the Davies family, who understood how important people's dogs are to them. Today, this ethos continues and providing quality products that stand the test of time is a testament to the quality of their products.

Davies recognise that an appropriate diet is essential for a healthy dog. Key nutrients include protein, oils/fats, carbohydrate, fibre, vitamins and minerals. The foods have been carefully formulated to provide the required levels of these for optimum health. Some dogs are more sensitive than others to ingredients such as wheat gluten, dairy, beef and soya. Davies range has been formulated without these ingredients, making this hypoallergenic product ideal for dogs with sensitive stomachs. The grain free range is being introduced and no added colours are used. In addition, dogs will benefit from added prebiotics for digestive health and omegas 3 and 6 for skin and coat health.
