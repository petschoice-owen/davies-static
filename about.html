About Davies

The Original Davies Since 1881

Established in 1881, the Original Davies pet food range of products has been developed with over 100 years experience in manufacturing pet foods. As a privately owned company we enjoy the freedom to listen to our customers and utilise the latest science in developing products that are beneficial to your dog.

At Pets Choice Ltd we understand your needs and expectations from a food whether it be for a pet, working, racing or show dog - the food must perform and meet your demands. By listening to your needs and utilising your experience in conjunction with our technology and dedicated expert staff, we know we can deliver wholesome, nutritious foods, enabling you to achieve the maximum health and performance from your dog.
